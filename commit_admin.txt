## Architecture:

All three endpoints follow the Redis-first, DB-fallback pattern:
1. Try persistent Redis cache (states:meta, districts:meta HASHes)
2. On cache miss or error, fall back to PostgreSQL
3. Never fail due to Redis errors
4. Comprehensive logging for monitoring
