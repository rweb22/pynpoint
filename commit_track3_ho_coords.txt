## Why Head Office instead of Centroid?

### Problem with Centroid:
- Centroid = geometric center of pincode boundary polygon
- May be in the middle of nowhere (parks, rivers, etc.)
- Not useful for logistics/delivery distance calculations

### Solution: Head Office Coordinates
- Head Office (HO) = main post office for the pincode
- Actual physical building with real GPS coordinates
- Represents the true delivery hub for the area
- Much more accurate for logistics distance calculations
